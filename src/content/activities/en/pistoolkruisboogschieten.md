Pistol crossbow shooting combines the best of both worlds: the precision of a crossbow with the handiness of a pistol. This unique activity offers an exciting challenge for anyone who wants to experience this special form of shooting sport.

Under the guidance of experienced instructors, participants learn the basic techniques of pistol crossbow shooting. Safety is the top priority, and all participants receive comprehensive instruction in handling the pistol crossbow.

After the basic training, various exercises and challenges follow where participants can test their skills. The activity often concludes with a competition between participants, which adds extra excitement and promotes team building.
